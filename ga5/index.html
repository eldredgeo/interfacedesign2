Job Details
Generac's Intern position is responsible for working with the Engineering Systems teams to drive new technologies, processes and techniques across the global footprint. Under supervision of the team leaders, the intern will create and assist in developing new technologies or techniques for product design, develop or revise work instructions or procedures for designers/engineers to follow, and potentially develop and deliver technology training courses to the engineering community.
Qualifications
Minimal Qualifications: Currently Pursuing a BS degree (preferably in Electrical or Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or UX) Preferred Qualifications: Full time during the summer and the ability to continue during the school year for 15-20 hours per week.

Knowledge/Skills: Understanding or working knowledge of SharePoint, Creo, SolidWorks, and/or AutoCAD experienceUnderstanding of PLM/ERP systemsMicrosoft Office Experience (including Excel) Physical demands: While the employee is performing the duties of the this job, they are required to talk, hear, use hands to manipulate objects or controls, stand and walk, stoop, bend or reach above shoulders and lift up to 25 pounds.
